Realtime Traffic Alerts

Stop being the last to know why your bus isn't moving. MoveIQ provides instant notifications about major disruptions on your specific corridor before you even head to the bus stop.

Whether it's a sudden roadblock, a broken-down vehicle, or an unexpected fare spike, we push the most critical updates directly to you.

By focusing on "Early Warning," we help you avoid the stress of the unknown and reclaim the hours of your day.

Know the road, before you're on the road.

Live Pulse Feed

The Live Pulse Feed is the "heartbeat" of your commute. This chronological stream gives you a second-by-second look at the ground truth on major high-traffic routes.

Unlike traditional maps that only show red lines, the Pulse Feed tells you why there's a delay — featuring verified descriptions from people standing right at the scene.

It's a transparent, live dashboard that ensures you never have to "wait blindly" again.

Community Traffic Reports

MoveIQ is built on the power of the community. With our simple "2-tap" reporting system, you can warn thousands of fellow commuters about a delay in seconds.

Our survey shows that 94% of users report because they want to help others avoid the same pain they've experienced.

Every report is verified by the community through an upvote system, ensuring the data stays fresh and honest.

When we look out for each other, everyone gets home faster.
