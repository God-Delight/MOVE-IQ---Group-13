Frequently asked questions

Why should I report an incident?

When you report an incident by taking 2 seconds to report a delay, you are helping thousands of fellow commuters avoid the same situation. In turn, you benefit from the "ground truth" reported by others before you learn about their routes. It's about commuters looking out for commuters because when we share what we see, everyone gets home faster.

What exactly does MoveIQ do?

MoveIQ is a community-driven traffic reporting platform that provides real-time updates on road conditions, incidents, and traffic situations across major Nigerian routes.

How is this different from Google Maps or Waze?

Unlike Google Maps or Waze, MoveIQ is built specifically for Nigerian roads and commuters, with hyperlocal incident reporting and a community focused on Nigerian routes.

How do I know the information is accurate?

All reports are verified through a community upvote system. The more people confirm an incident, the more reliable it becomes. Outdated reports are automatically flagged.

Do I need to pay to use MoveIQ?

No, MoveIQ is completely free to download and use. Our mission is to make commuting better for everyone.

Which routes are currently supported?

We currently support 10 major high-density transit routes across Lagos and Abuja, with plans to expand to more cities soon.

Does the app use a lot of battery or data?

MoveIQ is optimized to use minimal data and battery. It only fetches updates when you're actively using the app or when a critical alert needs to be pushed to you.
