About Us

What makes us 'Move' you may ask?

MOVE IQ is a smart commuter navigation platform designed to help people move through the city more efficiently. By combining real-time traffic insights with community-powered incident reports, we help commuters make better decisions on the road. Our goal is to make everyday travel simpler, faster, and less stressful.

Our Mission

Our mission is to help commuters spend less time stuck in traffic and more time reaching their destinations. MOVE IQ aims to provide reliable route information, live traffic updates, and practical insights that make daily commuting smarter and more predictable.

Why We Built Move IQ

Urban commuting can be unpredictable. Traffic congestion, accidents, road construction, and checkpoints often disrupt daily travel plans. MOVE IQ was built to give commuters better visibility into what's happening on the road and help them choose the smartest route. By allowing users to report incidents in real time, the platform creates a shared network of information that benefits everyone on the road.

Frequently asked questions

Why should I report an incident?

MoveIQ runs on the "Social Contract" of the road. By taking 2 seconds to report a delay, you are helping thousands of fellow commuters avoid the same frustration. In turn, you benefit from the "ground truth" reported by others before you even leave your house. It's about commuters looking out for commuters — because when we share what we see, everyone gets home faster.

What exactly does MoveIQ do?

MoveIQ is a community-driven traffic reporting platform that provides real-time updates on road conditions, incidents, and traffic situations across major Nigerian routes.

How is this different from Google Maps or Waze?

Unlike Google Maps or Waze, MoveIQ is built specifically for Nigerian roads and commuters, with hyperlocal incident reporting and a community focused on Nigerian routes.

How do I know the information is accurate?

All reports are verified through a community upvote system. The more people confirm an incident, the more reliable it becomes.

Do I need to pay to use MoveIQ?

No, MoveIQ is completely free to download and use.

Which routes are currently supported?

We currently support 10 major high-density transit routes across Lagos and Abuja.

Does the app use a lot of battery or data?

MoveIQ is optimized to use minimal data and battery.
